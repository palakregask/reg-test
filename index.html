Environmental Protection and Emissions Regulations — REG/2020-45

Section 12 — Emission Reporting Obligations

Current to 2025-11-01 · Last amended 2025-08-15 · Previous versions

Part 5, Division 2 — Annual Reporting Requirements

Note: This section applies to all licensed operators of Class A and Class B industrial facilities as defined under section 4 of these Regulations.

Obligation to Report

12 Every operator of a licensed facility must submit an annual emissions report to the Minister no later than March 31 of each year, covering the preceding calendar year.
(2) The report referred to in subsection (1) must include
(a) the total volume of each regulated substance released into the atmosphere, expressed in tonnes;
(b) the monitoring methodology used and the name of any accredited third-party verifier;
(c) a comparison of reported emissions against the facility's permitted thresholds under Schedule 4; and
(d) any corrective actions taken in response to exceedances identified in the previous reporting period.
(3) An operator who fails to submit the report within the time prescribed by subsection (1) is liable to an administrative penalty as set out in Schedule 4.

Format and Submission

13 Reports must be submitted electronically using the National Emissions Registry portal in the format prescribed by the Minister.
(2) Where technical difficulties prevent electronic submission, an operator may submit a paper report accompanied by a written explanation. The Minister may, at their discretion, accept or reject such a submission.

Certification

14 Every report must be certified by a senior officer of the operator as being accurate and complete to the best of their knowledge.
(2) A person who certifies a report knowing it to be false or misleading commits an offence under section 130 of these Regulations.
